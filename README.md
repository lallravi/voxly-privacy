# Voxly Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 9, 2026

Voxly does not collect, store, or share any personal information.

All tasks are stored locally on your device only. No data is sent to any server.

Voxly uses your device microphone solely for speech recognition. Audio is never recorded or transmitted.

We do not use analytics, tracking, or advertising.
